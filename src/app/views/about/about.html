Vicky

Vicky's Little Vikings is run by me, Vicky. I have worked in childcare as a nanny for 20 years.

I obviously love children and have done since a young age. I started working part-time as a nanny in 1994 whilst at six-form in Norfolk. I later became the family's full-time nanny and moveed with them to London.

Since then, I have nannied for 14 children (and parents!), potty trained a dozen and have remained good friends with all them (See references on home page).
