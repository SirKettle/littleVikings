It is my absolute priority to get all babies and children to safety in the case of a fire, while they are in my care as quickly and as safely as possible.

I have in place two smoke detectors, one in the kitchen and one in the hallway, which I check regularly.

In the kitchen above the hob I have in place at all times a fire blanket, which would be replaced if ever used.

Behind the kitchen door I have a small fire extinguisher which I will only use in an emergency.

If there is a fire:

  • First I will get the babies and children to a place of safety as quickly and safely as possible.
  • I will have a meeting point outside of the concierge desk of New river village.
  • I will ring 999 for the fire brigade.
  • I will reassure all babies and children in my care.
  • Ring and inform all parents.
